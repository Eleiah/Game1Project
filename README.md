>0 - floor

>1 - wall 1

>2 - wall 2

>3 - wall 3

>4 - hero

>5 - switch

>6 - traps

>7 - water

>8 - lava

>9 - planks

>'>100 - enemies

  >>11 - wall 2

  >>13 - wall 4
	
  >>14 - water
	
  >>15 - lava
	
  >>16 -
